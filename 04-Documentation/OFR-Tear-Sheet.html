One Firm Risk Issue Tracker

A fully M365-native operational risk tracking application designed to capture, triage, track, and resolve cross-firm risk issues. Built entirely on the Microsoft 365 stack with zero third-party dependencies.

Application Screens
01

Dashboard

Executive KPI overview with 5 color-coded metric cards (Open, Stale, High, Medium, Low priority counts), live Intake Queue with inline Intake Review panel for accepting or rejecting pending issues, and a prominent "+ Submit New Issue" button.

02

Issue Tracker

Full issue list with search/filter, traffic-light staleness coding, and an inline quick-update side panel for changing status and adding notes without leaving the screen.

03

Issue Detail

Structured issue header with ID, owner, priority, status and dates. Chronological update history timeline with card entries and an Add Update form.

04

Submit Issue

Self-service issue submission form with title, description, and priority fields. Auto-captures submitter identity and writes directly to the Intake Queue.
